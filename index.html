Simulations

Empathetics delivers a range of AI-powered simulations built from the ground up for education. Each simulation is grounded in proven learning science—using scaffolding, staged learning, and spaced repetition to help students gain confidence and retain knowledge.

Student tutors

Safe, low-risk environments where learners can ask questions, test ideas, and build understanding—always aligned to your courses and programs.

Task-based activities

Dynamic short-answer and essay-style tasks that double as worksheets or tutorials, with immediate feedback for synchronous or self-paced learning.

High-fidelity simulations

Rich, scenario-driven cases that mirror clinical encounters, preparing learners for authentic decision-making.
