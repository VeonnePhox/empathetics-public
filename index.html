Learning needs practice

Traditional teaching methods don't scale practice.

Critical reasoning and professional communication are mastered by doing: conducting an interview, asking the right questions, and responding efficiently. But instructor-led, supervised practice is resource-intensive and hard to scale.

  • Class sizes outgrow the staff who can give feedback
  • Placement and simulation time is limited and costly
  • Feedback often arrives too late to change behaviour
Practice made easy

Empathetics scales practice.

Build simulated interviews and interactions from your own course material. Let students practise and get feedback as a self-paced learning activity — by text or voice.

  • Self-paced, realistic practice for every learner
  • Automatic, rubric-based feedback on every attempt
  • A usage dashboard showing attempts, completion, and cohort progress
Real conversations

Students talk or messages. It responds — by voice or text.

No multiple choice, no canned branching dialogue. Get students into an actual conversation, and Empathetics will respond like the patient, client, or tutor it's built to simulate.

  • Near-real-time voice conversation, with transcription
  • Or text-based messaging if that suits the task better
  • Automatically give individualised, rubric-based feedback after conversation
