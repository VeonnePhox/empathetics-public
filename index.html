Pilot model

A practical pathway from idea to institutional pilot.

The strongest starting point is usually a defined teaching need, a specific learner group, and a small set of target activities that can be piloted, evaluated, and refined.

01

Define the teaching problem

Choose the communication, reasoning, or practice gap that matters most for your pilot cohort.

02

Shape pilot experiences

Design a focused set of simulations or feedback experiences that align with your curriculum and support repeatable practice.

03

Run with a real learner group

Pilot with a defined cohort and gather implementation insights from staff and learners.

04

Review the path to scale

Use pilot outcomes to decide whether to iterate, expand, integrate more deeply, or explore broader adoption.
