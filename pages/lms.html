LMS Integrations

Seamlessly connect Empathetics simulations to your organisation's exisiting LMS — including Moodle, Blackboard, Canvas, and Brightspace.

No extra logins. No duplicate data. Just immersive learning, directly where your students already are.

Single Sign-On (SSO)

Launch Empathetics directly from your LMS course using LTI 1.3 / Advantage standards. Students and staff authenticate securely via your institution’s login — no new accounts needed.

SCORM & xAPI Ready

Empathetics supports SCORM and xAPI (Experience API) for deep analytics and interoperability with institutional LRS systems — enabling outcome tracking and research.

Privacy & Security

Fully compliant with LTI 1.3, GDPR, and OAuth2 / JWT standards. Data is securely hosted on isolated instances and dedicated infrastructure to comply with organisational requirements.
