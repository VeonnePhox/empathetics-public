Free public access

Clinichat is the free public distro of Empathetics.

Clinichat gives people a simple way to sign up, explore clinical cases, and come back for new releases. It is the open, public-facing experience of the Empathetics platform.

Free to Join

Clinichat is free right now. The goal at this stage is to grow usage, learn from engagement, and make the experience easy to access.

Regular Case Releases

New cases can be released on a regular basis, giving users a reason to return and explore a growing public library of experiences.

Built on Empathetics

Clinichat is not a separate product story. It showcases the same underlying capability in a lightweight, public format.

What People Can Explore

The page does not need to explain every detail of the system. It only needs to show enough capability for someone to understand why Clinichat is worth trying.

Case-based Conversations

Explore scenario-driven interactions that let users work through different clinical situations, responses, and communication challenges.

Low-friction Entry

Clinichat is designed for discovery. People arriving from Instagram or other public channels should be able to understand it quickly and start without institutional setup.

A Living Public Library

Ongoing releases help Clinichat function as a public showcase, a learning environment, and a source of insight into what people engage with most.

A Path Into Empathetics

For educators, partners, or institutions, Clinichat also signals what the broader Empathetics platform can support in more tailored environments.
