How to Update or Extend Styles

  1. Colours: Defined in :root inside _layouts/default.html. Add or modify any --brand variable, then reference with bg-[var(--newcolor)] or text-[var(--newcolor)].
  2. Typography: Classes like .heading-1, .heading-2, and .body-text are also declared in default.html under “Formatting styles”. Move these into /assets/css/typography.css for site-wide use if preferred.
  3. Usage: Use the custom classes when writing content, e.g. <h1 class="heading-1">Page Title</h1> or <p class="body-text">Paragraph content</p>.
  4. Dark Mode: All styles support the .dark class automatically. Test with your browser’s dark mode toggle.
