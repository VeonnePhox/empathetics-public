Research & Adoption Support

Empathetics is designed to grow with you — whether you’re introducing AI-based simulations into your curriculum or embedding them in clinical education programs. Our team provides end-to-end guidance across the process:

Research Enablement

  • ✔ Study design & ethics application support
  • ✔ Data collection & analytics integration
  • ✔ Co-authored publications & dissemination

Adoption & Implementation

  • ✔ Curriculum mapping & educator onboarding
  • ✔ IT and LMS integration guidance
  • ✔ Continuous feedback and iteration cycles

We’re here to help you every step of the way — from the first idea to measurable impact.
